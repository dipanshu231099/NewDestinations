Dharamshala

Itinerary 5



Day 1


Take a mode of travel so that you land early in the morning on Day 1. Stay in Dharamsala. Visit the following places in the order of mention:

  • Gyuto Monastery. (beautiful monastery in verdant surroundings)
  • Norbulingka Institute.
  • Akhanjar Mahadev temple ( Take a dip in the alpine brook behind the temple)
  • Cricket stadium.
  • Martyr’s Memorial.
  • Tea Garden Kunal pathri temple (for sunset)
  • Kotwali bazar for shopping/curios.

Day 2


Leave early for triund so as to return the same evening. Stay in M’cleodganj. 14kms from M’cleodganj (1 way) want to shorten this? Have a taxi drop you at Gallu. The distance reduces to 9 km.

Day 3


On day 3 visit the following places in order: St John’s church forsythganj, Dal Lake, M’cleodganj, Bhagsu nag, Waterfall, Evening atCafe Illerratti on Jogiwara road.
